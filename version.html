This page describes the current beta version of WhiteBox Agency and provides a glimpse into our roadmap. We’re committed to giving you full control over your browsing experience by actively monitoring and blocking unwanted content across all websites.

Filtering Levels

The extension currently offers three filtering levels so you can choose the protection that’s right for you:

  • None: Filtering is disabled. Use this mode when you don’t need any protection or want to quickly switch the extension off.
  • Moderate: Balances safety and browsing experience. WhiteBox Agency blocks obviously inappropriate or sexual content while allowing most ordinary content through. Recommended for general browsing.
  • Strict: Aggressively filters questionable content using more sensitive heuristics and AI detection. This mode is best for parents, educators and anyone seeking maximum protection.

Future Revisions

As we continue to develop WhiteBox Agency, we plan to add more advanced customization and smarter detection. Upcoming releases will include features like:

  • Adaptive filtering that learns from your preferences over time
  • Enhanced AI models for detecting context and nuance
  • Detailed reporting and analytics to help you understand what’s being filtered
  • Integration with family and classroom management tools

Vision

Our vision is simple: you take back control. WhiteBox Agency gives you an active role in fighting back against unwanted content so you’re not at the mercy of whatever pops up on your screen. We believe that with transparent and customizable filtering, you can enjoy the internet on your terms.
