About WhiteBox Agency

WhiteBox Agency is a browser extension for Chrome and Edge that filters sexually explicit or inappropriate content from your browsing experience. Designed for families, educators, and anyone who wants more control over what appears on screen, WhiteBox Agency empowers you to craft your own safe corner of the internet.

How WhiteBox Agency Works

WhiteBox Agency is built for real-life browsing. Instead of blocking whole websites, it steps in as you browse and cleanly “white‑boxes” unwanted content—so the rest of the page can stay usable.

1) Detect

Scans the page as it loads and as you scroll, looking at images, text, and surrounding context.

2) Decide

Uses fast on‑device heuristics first, then AI‑assisted checks when needed—without storing your browsing history.

3) White‑Box

Replaces detected content with clean placeholders so you stay in control of what appears on your screen.

What Sets WhiteBox Agency Apart

Unlike traditional filters that rely solely on predefined blocklists of domains or URLs, WhiteBox Agency actively scans the content of every page you visit and makes intelligent decisions about what you see. It uses a mix of heuristics and AI methods to detect and white‑box unwanted content—including images and text—on sites that are not blocked by normal filters. This means you’re protected even on new or obscure sites, and you stay in control of your browsing experience.

FAQ

Does WhiteBox Agency track me?

No. WhiteBox Agency does not store browsing history. Filtering runs on your device.

Will it block entire sites?

WhiteBox Agency focuses on blocking unwanted content on a page while keeping the rest usable.

What if it misses something (or blocks too much)?

Early access means improvements are ongoing. Use the Feedback page to report misses or false positives.

Can I change strictness?

Yes—None, Moderate, and Strict are designed for different sensitivity levels.

Installation & Setup

Installing on Google Chrome

1. Visit the WhiteBox Agency extension page on the Chrome Web Store.
2. Click Add to Chrome and confirm the installation when prompted.
3. After it installs, pin the WhiteBox Agency icon from the extensions menu for quick access.

Installing on Microsoft Edge

1. Go to the Microsoft Edge Add‑ons store and search for “WhiteBox Agency”.
2. Click Get (or Add to Edge) and approve the prompts to install.
3. Once installed, ensure WhiteBox Agency is enabled in your Edge extensions list.
