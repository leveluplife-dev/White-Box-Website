Limitations & roadmap

White Box is currently built and maintained by one person. That means I move fast, but I also have to prioritize the most impactful improvements first. Pro subscribers directly fund the runway to expand coverage, reduce misses, and ultimately hire help.

What White Box may miss today

  • Some GIFs, depending on how they’re embedded
  • Some embedded video players and complex in‑page media
  • Content that is very small, heavily stylized, or rapidly changing
  • Rare site layouts that break normal page patterns
Good news:
The roadmap is expandable. With enough supporters, this evolves into broader media protection, smarter models, fewer false positives, and stronger coverage across the modern web.

Roadmap direction

  • Better GIF and embedded video coverage
  • Improved detection accuracy (fewer misses)
  • Stronger handling for edge cases and tricky layouts
  • More user controls (whitelists, sensitivity tuning, reporting tools)
  • Optional device and family‑friendly controls (long‑term)

How you can help

  • Try Pro to fund development
  • Use the feedback page to report misses or false positives
  • Share White Box with a friend who wants more control online

If you ever feel stuck or something isn’t working as expected, please reach out via the feedback page — I read every submission.
