Privacy Policy

Plain‑English summary of how WhiteBox Agency handles data.

We don’t store your browsing data

WhiteBox Agency performs filtering on your device. We do not collect or store your browsing history, the pages you visit, or the content you view.

What we may store locally (on your device)

  • Your chosen filter setting (None / Moderate / Strict)
  • Your allowlist / blocklist preferences (if you use them)

Accounts (website)

If you create an account on this website, we store the info you submit (e.g., email) for account access and product updates. We don’t sell your personal information.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your account data. No system is perfect—please use a strong, unique password.
